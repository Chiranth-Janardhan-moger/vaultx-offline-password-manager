VaultX is a fully offline, privacy-first password manager that keeps your passwords secure on your device. Your data never leaves your phone and never touches the cloud.

KEY FEATURES:
• 100% Offline - No internet connection required or used
• AES-256 Encryption - Military-grade security for your passwords
• Biometric Unlock - Fingerprint and face unlock support
• Autofill Service - Seamlessly fill passwords in other apps
• Smart Categories - Automatically organizes passwords by service type
• Master Password - Generate deterministic passwords from a master password
• Export/Import - Encrypted backup and restore functionality
• Dark/Light Themes - Multiple theme options with accessibility support
• PIN Protection - 6-digit PIN as backup unlock method
• Auto-lock Timer - Configurable inactivity timeout

PRIVACY & SECURITY:
• Zero network permissions - Your data stays on your device
• No analytics or tracking
• No cloud storage or syncing
• Open source and auditable
• Encrypted local storage only
• Screenshot protection in sensitive screens

AUTOFILL SUPPORT:
• Android Autofill Service integration
• Works with browsers and apps
• Secure authentication before filling
• No data sent to external services

VaultX is perfect for users who prioritize privacy and want complete control over their password data. All encryption and decryption happens locally on your device using industry-standard algorithms.

The app requires minimal permissions:
• Biometric authentication for secure unlock
• Autofill service for password filling
• Vibration for haptic feedback
• Notifications for clipboard password detection

No internet, storage, or location permissions are required or requested.
